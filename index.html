Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed by Inspect AI Pro ("we," "our," or "us"). This policy applies to our mobile application and related services.

1. Age Requirement (18+)

Inspect AI Pro is strictly intended for individuals who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 18. If you are under 18, you are not permitted to use this application.

2. Information Collection and Use

To provide you with our multimodal AI features, we require access to certain device functions. The app processes data primarily on your device or transmits it securely to our trusted service providers for real-time analysis:

  • Camera and Image Data: The app requires access to your device's camera to utilize Object Recognition and OCR (Text Extraction) features. Images captured are transmitted securely to Google's Gemini AI API solely for the purpose of generating your inspection results. We do not store your images on our servers.
  • Location Data (GPS): If you enable the Smart GPS Context feature, we collect your real-time location data to provide geographical context to your scans. This data is attached to your local files and is not tracked or stored remotely by us.
  • Audio Output: The app utilizes audio output for reading text and descriptions aloud.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, provide backend infrastructure, or serve advertisements. These services operate under their own Privacy Policies:

  • Google Play Services: For core app functionality.
  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob automatically collects certain device information, such as your Advertising ID, IP address, and usage data, to serve relevant ads.
  • Google Firebase: Used for app analytics, performance monitoring, and secure backend routing.
  • Google Gemini API: Used to process image and text data for our core AI functionalities.

4. Log Data and Analytics

Whenever you use our Service, in a case of an error in the app, we collect data and information on your phone called Log Data. This may include information such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

5. Security

We value your trust in providing your information. We use commercially acceptable means of protecting it, utilizing encrypted API transmissions. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. User Rights & Ad Opt-Out

Depending on your location (such as the UK, EEA, or California), you have specific privacy rights regarding your data. Because we do not require user accounts or store personal data on our servers, we do not hold identifiable profiles and therefore cannot manually delete individual analytics. However, you can manage or opt out of personalized advertising and tracking at any time by navigating to your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
