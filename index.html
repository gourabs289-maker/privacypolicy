Privacy Policy for Inspect AI Pro

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use Inspect AI Pro. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure, premium experience. Inspect AI Pro does not track you, does not serve advertisements, and does not permanently store your personal data.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

To provide our advanced visual intelligence and translation services, Inspect AI Pro requires access to certain device capabilities. All data processed by our app is handled ephemerally, meaning it is processed in real-time to deliver your results and is immediately discarded. We do not store this data on our servers.

  • Camera & Photos: The core functionality of Inspect AI Pro (Object Inspect and Text Inspect) requires camera access. Images captured are sent securely via the internet to our cloud AI service provider to generate object descriptions, encyclopedic data, and translations. These images are not saved to any external database.
  • Location Data (Precise & Approximate): The app may request location permissions to display localized context (such as your current street name, city, and pin code) directly on your screen. This location data is processed locally and ephemerally; it is not tracked, recorded, or shared with third-party advertisers.
  • Internet & Downloads: The app requires an active internet connection to communicate with our AI servers. It downloads the generated text, translations, and analysis results back to your device seamlessly.
  • Audio & Microphone (If applicable): If you utilize voice-input features or audio read-aloud functions, audio data is strictly used for real-time speech-to-text or text-to-speech processing. It is never recorded or stored.
  • Language Preferences: We process your selected language settings to deliver accurate translations and regional encyclopedia results.

2. Third-Party Service Providers

Because Inspect AI Pro relies on advanced Artificial Intelligence, we use secure, industry-standard third-party cloud APIs (such as Google/Gemini services) to process your images, text, and voice requests. These providers act strictly as service providers to facilitate the app's core functionality. They are bound by strict data processing agreements and do not use your ephemeral data to build individual advertising profiles.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Inspect AI Pro does not require user accounts, logins, or passwords. Because we process your camera scans and location queries ephemerally, we do not retain or store your personal data. Therefore, there is no stored data to delete, and no user deletion request forms are necessary.

4. Children's Privacy

Inspect AI Pro is a premium utility application intended for general audiences and professionals. It is strictly targeted to users who are 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to ensure its immediate deletion.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our app's features or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted directly on this page, and the "Effective Date" at the top will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
